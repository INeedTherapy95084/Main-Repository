Article on HTML:

HTML, or Hypertext Markup Language, serves as the foundation of the World Wide Web, defining the structure and content of web pages. Using a system of tags, HTML allows creators to organize information, embed images and multimedia, and establish hyperlinks, shaping the digital landscape we navigate daily. Its simplicity and versatility make it accessible to beginners while providing advanced capabilities for seasoned developers. As the backbone of the internet, HTML continues to evolve, adapting to new technologies and trends, ensuring the seamless delivery of content across devices and platforms.
